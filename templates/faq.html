Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set a budget?

Go to the Dashboard and click on "Set Budget" to enter your total budget.

How do I add an expense?

Go to the Dashboard and click on "Add Expense" to enter the details of your expense.

Can I reset my budget?

Yes, you can reset your budget from the Profile page.

How do I delete my account?

Go to the Profile page and click on "Delete Account".

How do I change my profile picture?

Go to the Profile page and click on your profile image to upload a new one.

How do I view my previous budgets?

Go to the "Previous Records" page to view all your previous budgets and expenses.
